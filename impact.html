The wine is yours, but we are borrowing the bottle! Did you know that 70% of wine’s carbon footprint comes from the glass bottles? 1% FOR THE PLANET

In 2002, Yvon Chouinard, founder of Patagonia, and Craig Mathews, founder of Blue Ribbon Flies, started a global movement of businesses giving 1% of their sales back to the environment. Today, Revelshine is proud to be one of the 3,000 members of 1% for the Planet.
