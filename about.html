4TH GENERATION WINE MAKER

JAKE BILBRO | FOUNDER

Lead by a fourth-generation Sonoma County winemaker and grape grower, with a passion for the wine lifestyle that began at a very young age when his chores were cleaning barrels, dragging hoses, and in most ways, being his dad’s shadow. Living amongst the vines and wines of Sonoma County ignited a relationship and bond with vineyards, wine, dirt, food, and the seasonally minded community of people.

PAIRING TWO LOVES TOGETHER

Wine, in its truest form is a story, a living thing, art. And to make something of real substance requires inspiration. Jake learned from his dad that winemaking at its heart is living a lifestyle that inspires creativity, life, storytelling, and love in wild outdoor places that one then uses to create real and delicious wine.

PAIRING TWO LOVES TOGETHER

Jake created Revelshine for special moments spent with loved ones creating memories, and for moments of tranquility in special places. As humans, we are connected to each other, ourselves, and the planet.
